# Filing a Police Report After a Car Accident

When you're involved in a car accident, knowing whether you need a police report and how to get one can feel overwhelming. A police report is an official document that records what happened. It can help with insurance claims and protect you legally. Here's what you need to know.

When You Need a Police Report

You must call police if anyone is injured or if there's significant damage to vehicles or property. Many states require a report for accidents involving injuries. Even if it's not legally required, getting a report is smart. It creates an official record of the accident that insurance companies will trust more than your word alone.

Call the police non-emergency line if no one is hurt. Use 911 only if someone needs medical help or there's a safety risk, like a car in traffic.

What to Do at the Scene

Before the police arrive, stay calm and move to a safe location if possible. Turn on your hazard lights. Take photos of all vehicle damage, the accident scene, road conditions, and license plates. Get names, phone numbers, and insurance information from the other driver and any witnesses.

When police arrive, be honest and stick to the facts. Describe what you saw, not what you think happened. Don't admit fault or apologize—insurance companies will determine who caused the accident. Answer the officer's questions clearly and completely.

Getting Your Report

Ask the officer for the report number and the police department's contact information. The report may not be ready immediately—sometimes it takes days or weeks. You can usually request it online or by calling the police department's non-emergency number.

Some reports require a small fee to obtain. Save the receipt. Send a copy to your insurance company right away when you file your claim.

Conclusion

A police report protects you by creating an official account of the accident. Take it seriously while at the scene, be truthful with officers, and follow up to get your copy. Having this documentation makes insurance claims smoother and gives you peace of mind knowing everything is properly documented.
