Common Questions

Are the calculators accurate?+
Our calculators use standard financial formulas and are designed to give accurate estimates. However, actual loan offers may vary based on your credit score, lender policies, and current market conditions. Always verify with your lender before making financial decisions.
Is the legal information on your site accurate?+
Our legal guides are written to be accurate and up-to-date, but they provide general information only — not legal advice. Laws vary by state and circumstances differ. Always consult a licensed attorney for advice specific to your situation.
How do you make money?+
FinGuide.net is supported by advertising through Google AdSense. Ads are served by Google based on your browsing history and our content. We do not receive payment for recommending specific lenders, attorneys, or insurers.
Can I suggest a new guide or calculator?+
Absolutely! We love hearing what our readers need. Send your suggestion using the form above or email us directly at info@finguide.net. We regularly add new content based on user requests.
