# How to Pay Off Your Mortgage Early

Paying off your home loan decades before the scheduled date is a smart financial goal. You'll save tens of thousands in interest and own your home outright sooner. Here are proven strategies to make it happen.

Make Larger Monthly Payments

The simplest way to pay off your mortgage early is to pay more each month. Even an extra $100 or $200 helps reduce your balance faster and saves significant interest over time. Talk to your lender to confirm there are no penalties for early repayment. Many people use tax refunds, bonuses, or raises to make these extra payments without straining their regular budget.

Use the Biweekly Payment Method

Instead of paying once a month, switch to paying half your mortgage payment every two weeks. Since there are 26 biweekly periods in a year, you'll make 13 half-payments instead of 12 full ones. This equals one extra full payment annually, which can shave years off your mortgage. Ask your lender if they offer a biweekly program, or simply make one extra payment each year on your own.

Refinance to a Shorter Loan Term

If interest rates drop, consider refinancing your 30-year mortgage into a 15-year loan. Your monthly payments will be higher, but you'll pay much less interest overall and own your home faster. Run the numbers first to make sure the new rate and closing costs make financial sense. This strategy works best if you can comfortably afford the higher payments.

Bonus Strategy: Apply Windfalls to Principal

Whenever you receive unexpected money—an inheritance, work bonus, or side income—put it directly toward your mortgage principal. This accelerates your payoff without affecting your normal monthly budget. Even small amounts add up quickly and significantly reduce how much interest you'll pay.

Conclusion

Paying off your mortgage early requires commitment but delivers substantial rewards. Choose one strategy that fits your budget, or combine multiple approaches for faster results. Start today, stay consistent, and you'll be mortgage-free well ahead of schedule. Your future self will thank you for the financial freedom and peace of mind.
